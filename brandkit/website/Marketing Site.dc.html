HOW IT WORKS

A read-only sidecar, next to AMS

STEP 1

docker compose up -d

Pulse + ClickHouse start on your host. Helm chart available for Kubernetes.

STEP 2

Point it at AMS

One URL + token. Read-only REST polling begins; streams appear within 10 s.

STEP 3

Drop in the beacon

One script tag in your player adds viewer-side QoE: startup, rebuffer, bitrate.

STEP 4

Route the alerts

Email, Slack, Telegram, PagerDuty or webhooks — with grouping, mute and maintenance windows.

BUILT FOR THE AMS ECOSYSTEM

Not a bolt-on. A module.

Pulse speaks AMS natively — applications, broadcasts, WebRTC client stats, cluster nodes — across v2.8 through 3.x, standalone or clustered. It tolerates version variance and needs zero changes to your streaming stack.

  • Auto-discovers AMS applications and cluster nodes (≤30 s)
  • Origin/edge aware — viewer counts deduplicated across the cluster
  • Per-source webhook secrets for multi-AMS fleets
  • Cookie-session and bearer auth, TLS-verified
  • Works with the AMS marketplace: OEMs and agencies can white-label
