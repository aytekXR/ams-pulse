# poem

*for Pulse — a quiet watcher of streams*

A pulse beneath the broadcast hum,
where viewers gather, come and go;
each tenant keeps its own small drum,
each frame a heartbeat in the flow.

When rebuffers rise or bitrates fall,
a rule wakes softly, sends its word —
no blended noise, no cross-tenant call,
just one clear signal, cleanly heard.

Ship, then verify; roll it slow;
green checks, a smoke, then let it run.
The dashboards breathe, the metrics glow,
the work, for now, is quietly done.
